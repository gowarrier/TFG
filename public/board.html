TFG Executive Board

The TFG Executive Board oversees the operations of the four TFG child organizations. The Executive Board is composed of the presidents and vice-presidents of each child organization, as well as five members of the TFG cabinet. The Executive Board seeks to constantly improve operations through coordination, reducing redundancies and promoting synergies.
