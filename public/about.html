Mission Statement Who We Are

The Tartan Finance Group (TFG) is Carnegie Mellon University's undergraduate finance association. We work every day to help nurture students' interests in finance and guide them towards future careers. We primarily operate through our 4 child organizations:

  • Introductory Finance Seminars (IFS)
  • Investment Banking Academy (IBA)
  • Sales & Trading Academy (STA)
  • Tartan Student Fund (TSF)

TFG is committed to helping students at all points of their CMU experience, from the freshman looking for a gentle introduction to corporate finance and valuation, to the junior seeking resources to help kickstart their career in finance, to the senior looking for a platform to gain real experience in equity investing.

Merger Why TFG?

Before the Spring of 2016, the divisions of TFG operated independently. While each organization enjoyed success on their own, it became clear that we would be even stronger together. By coordinating with each other, TFG is able to provide a seamless experience as students transition through the stages of their young financial lives.
